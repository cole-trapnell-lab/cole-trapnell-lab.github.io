Trapnell Lab
The Trapnell Lab at the University of Washington's Department of Genome Sciences studies cell differentiation, reprogramming, and other transitions between stable or metastable cellular states. We also study the role of cell-cell communication in governing these processes. We aim to identify genes that control cellular transitions, primarily using single-cell genomics.
